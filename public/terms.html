Short version: use Cordfol honestly, don't put anything illegal on your page, and understand that role verification only works while Discord lets us check it.

Your account

You need a Discord account to use Cordfol. Your handle (cordfol.org/yourname) is first-come, first-served. We may reclaim handles that impersonate other people, brands, or sit unused on clearly squatted names.

Your content

Whatever you put on your profile — bio, links, images, music — is yours, and you're responsible for it. Don't post anything illegal, hateful, or designed to scam people. We remove profiles that do, without warning if it's serious.

Verification

Verified badges reflect what Discord's API tells us at the time of the check. If you leave a server or lose a role, the badge goes away on the next re-check. Verification is proof of a role, not an endorsement of the person holding it.

Pro

Pro is a one-time purchase that unlocks extra themes and analytics. If we ever discontinue a Pro feature, we'll say so up front. Payments aren't refundable once the features have been used, but if something's broken, talk to us and we'll sort it out.

The service

Cordfol depends on Discord's API, which we don't control. We aim for high uptime but can't guarantee it, and we may change or discontinue features as the platform evolves.

Contact
